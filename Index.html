About this project

Age Calculator Pro exists because most age calculators either round things off or make you wait for a page reload just to see a number tick over. This one runs entirely in your browser, does calendar-accurate math — accounting for leap years and different month lengths — and updates live, down to the second.

Why we built it

A birthday isn't just a single number. It's years, but also months and days since the last one — and if you're curious, the number of seconds you've been alive right now. We wanted a tool that could answer all of those at once, clearly, without clutter.

How the math works

Your age in years, months, and days is calculated by comparing the current calendar date to your date of birth directly — not by dividing total days by 365.25. That means the "months" and "days" figures match what you'd count by hand on a calendar. The live counters (total days, hours, minutes, seconds) come from the exact millisecond difference between now and your birth date, refreshed once per second.

Fun extras — zodiac sign, estimated heartbeats, and milestone countdowns — are provided for entertainment and are approximate by nature.

Privacy, in short

Your date of birth is never sent to a server. Every calculation happens locally on your device. See the Privacy Policy below for full details.
